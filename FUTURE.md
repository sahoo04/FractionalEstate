# Future Roadmap

Planned features and improvements for FractionalEstate.

## Phase 1: Core Governance

**Governance System**
- DAO voting system using share ownership
- Token-weighted voting (1 share = 1 vote)
- Proposal creation and voting
- Execute approved proposals on-chain
- Delegate voting power to other addresses

Use case: Community votes on platform fee changes, new properties to add, fund allocation, etc.

## Phase 2: Privacy & Security

**Zero-Knowledge Proofs for KYC**
- Privacy-preserving identity verification
- Users prove they passed KYC without revealing personal data
- zk-SNARK based verification
- Stores only proof hash on-chain, not personal info
- Compliant with privacy regulations

Benefits:
- User privacy protected
- Still verifiable on-chain
- Prevents ID spoofing and duplicate accounts
- GDPR compliant

## Phase 3: Incentives & Engagement

**Loyalty Points System**
- Users earn points for:
  - Trading (buying/selling shares)
  - Holding shares long-term
  - Participating in governance
  - Community contributions
- Points redeemable for:
  - Marketplace fee discounts
  - Early access to new properties
  - Governance weight boost
  - Exclusive features

**Yield Farming**
- Staking rewards for long-term share holders
- APY increases with time held (loyalty bonus)
- Compound yields for continuous staking
- Auto-reinvestment option

Example:
- Hold 100 shares for 1 month: 2% APY
- Hold 100 shares for 6 months: 5% APY
- Hold 100 shares for 1 year: 8% APY

## Phase 4: Scalability & Expansion

**Property Fractionalization**

- Bulk property onboarding

**Automated Rent Collection**

**Multi-Chain Support**
- Deploy on Optimism (OP Mainnet)

- Unified dashboard across chains
